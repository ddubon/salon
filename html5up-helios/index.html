About Us

Biddy Hair Salon is East Boston's premier hair salon providing exceptional Coloring, Haircuts, Perming, Make-up, Blowouts, Nail services & Bridal Services to men and women. Our stylists have worked on photo shoots, weddings, sweets 16's and all other special occasions. We will work with you to create the hairstyle you have always wanted.

Established in 2005 with a the whole purpose of serving the East Boston community with quality service and a friendly customer environment. The owner, Ines Biddy, is professional with over 20 years of professional experience that is ready to help achieve the look you want.
